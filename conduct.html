Code of Conduct

Thank you for taking the time to consider volunteering in a Microsoft Research project. The purpose of this research is to build the most challenging and comprehensive global dataset of value-oriented questions and answers ever assembled, grounded in real-world scenarios rather than abstract thought experiments.

Procedures

If you choose to participate, you are invited to contribute value-related questions that current AI systems cannot answer reliably, focusing on human values, ethics, and moral judgment in real-life contexts.

During the study, we plan to collect information about you such as name/pseudonym, email address, title, field of research

Compensation

By participating in this study, you will receive

True North List: Each selected submission will receive RMB 500.

Insight List: Each selected submission will receive RMB 200.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Researchers will keep your participation and the information you share as confidential as possible.

Questions or Concerns: Contact the research team at reshma.ramprasad@microsoft.com
