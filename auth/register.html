Pre-Submission Confirmation

Thank you for taking the time to consider volunteering in a Microsoft Research project. The purpose of this research is to build the most challenging and comprehensive global dataset of value-oriented questions and answers ever assembled, grounded in real-world scenarios rather than abstract thought experiments. By collecting and curating these difficult cases, the Challenge seeks to:

(1)Advance research on value reasoning, moral judgment, and ethical alignment in advanced AI;
(2)Promote transparent, reproducible standards for value-related AI evaluation across academia and industry;
(3)Provide clearer moral and value-oriented guidance for the responsible development of future AI systems. Our long-term vision is to support the creation of AI agents that can offer value-based decision support in everyday contexts, to help users solve dilemmas and improve well-being, while operating in a safe, reliable, and responsible manner for the benefit of society.

Procedures

If you choose to participate, you are invited to contribute value-related questions that current AI systems cannot answer reliably, focusing on human values, ethics, and moral judgment in real-life contexts. Steps include:
(1)Complete online registration of Global AI Value Challenge and pass an eligibility review. Participants are required to use an institutional (university) email address.
(2) Create one original value-related question that challenges AI systems and provides a clear, well-justified answer (including an explanation to justify the answer's validity). Submit them through the submit page of Global AI Value Challenge website.
(3) Selected question–answer pairs will be included in the Challenge dataset. Accepted contributions will receive corresponding rewards and recognition.
In total, your participation will take approximately 30 minutes to complete registration, review the rules, consent materials, and become familiar with the submission system. The time required to prepare each question–answer pair will vary depending on your individual pace and the quality of the submission. No minimum time commitment is required beyond these activities.
During the study, we plan to collect information about you such as name/ pseudonym, email address, title, field of research

Compensation

By participating in this study, you will receive
(1)Monetary awards, based on the quality and impact of your contribution. Two award levels are available:
True North List: This includes accepted submissions that pose substantial challenges to large language models and best reflect directions related to human values and AI ethics. Each selected submission will receive RMB 500.
Insight List: This includes accepted submissions that demonstrate significant challenges and strong intellectual depth or insight. Each selected submission will receive RMB 200.
(2)An official Certificate of Participation in the MSRA Global AI Values Open Research Project.
(3)Public recognition as a contributor to this global AI values related dataset (using your name or a chosen pseudonym).
(4)Priority consideration for internship opportunities with the MSRA Societal AI group.
Award recipients will be contacted by the challenge organization within one month after the conclusion of the challenge, using the email address provided during registration, to confirm eligibility and arrange the specific details of award distribution.
